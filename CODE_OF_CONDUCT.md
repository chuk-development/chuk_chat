# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and inclusive
- No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Constructive feedback only
- Respect differing viewpoints

## Enforcement

Unacceptable behavior can be reported to support@chuk.dev

Violations may result in temporary or permanent bans from the project.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces: issues, PRs, discussions, and related communication channels.
